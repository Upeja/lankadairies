Yoghurt
  • Rank: Top 3 in Set Yoghurt in Sri Lanka.
  • Revenue Driver: #2 contributor to Lanka Dairies Limited’s topline.
  • Taste:Superior balance of sourness and sweetness.
  • Texture:Smooth and delightful.
In a milestone of dairy innovation, Ambewela Products (Pvt) Ltd introduced Ambewela Set Yoghurt in 2009. This 80g cup is celebrated for its rich flavour, velvety texture, and balanced nutritional profile.

Designed to suit different consumer needs, Ambewela Set Yoghurt is also available in a family pack variant, offering the same premium quality and taste in a larger, shareable format.
