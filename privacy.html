Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 April 2025

Introduction

This privacy statement provides information on the processing of personal data by Lanka Dairies Limited and its affiliated group companies (referred to as "Lanka Dairies", "we", "our" or "us").

"Personal data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual. This privacy statement does not apply to data about companies.

At Lanka Dairies, we take your privacy seriously. When you purchase our products, use our services, or interact with us, you may share personal data with us. The purpose of this privacy statement is to be upfront about the information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and how you can exercise your privacy rights, along with other relevant privacy-related information.

Please read this privacy statement carefully in conjunction with our website terms and conditions. Additionally, please review the privacy terms governing any specific campaigns, promotions, and contracts that you enter into with us to fully understand how we handle your personal data.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or wish to access or request corrections to any personal data we hold about you, please contact us (see ‘How to Contact Us’ below).

We may update or revise this privacy statement periodically. Any changes will be reflected here and will take effect either immediately or when we notify you. Continued engagement with us after such changes are published indicates your agreement to be bound by this privacy statement. This statement applies from 1 April 2025.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal data, including but not limited to:

• Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and any other information you provide voluntarily through contact forms, account registrations, or customer service interactions.

• Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, and browsing behavior on our website.

• Transaction Data: Payment details, purchase history, and billing information if you make transactions with us.

• Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking tools to analyze website traffic, customize content, and enhance your experience. You may adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies if preferred.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information may be used for the following purposes:

• To provide, maintain, and improve our website, services, and products.

• To process transactions, manage payments, and send purchase confirmations.

• To respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and enhance your user experience.

• To send promotional communications, newsletters, and service updates (you may opt out at any time).

• To conduct research, data analysis, and performance monitoring to improve our offerings.

• To comply with legal and regulatory obligations and prevent fraudulent activities.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. However, we may share data with:

• Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with website hosting, payment processing, analytics, and marketing services.

• Regulatory and Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights, safety, and compliance with legal obligations.

• Business Partners: If necessary, to facilitate relevant services or promotional offers in collaboration with trusted partners.

Data Protection and Security

We implement robust security measures, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we encourage users to take precautions when sharing personal data online.

Your Rights and Choices

You may have the following rights concerning your personal data:

• The right to access, update, or correct your information.

• The right to request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.

• The right to withdraw consent for certain processing activities.

• The right to opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at lakspray@lmfgroup.lk.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or opt out of tracking technologies.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites operated by third parties. Lanka Dairies Limited is not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal data.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage users to review this policy regularly for any updates.
