Innovation

At Lanka Dairies Limited, innovation is more than just progress—it is a commitment to quality, research, and animal health. Built on the strength of our heritage brands, we have consistently led Sri Lanka’s dairy industry, pioneering advancements in cutting-edge technology, sustainable practices, and scientific research.
Our approach focuses on expanding farm milk production, strengthening processing capabilities, and maintaining sustainability as a core pillar of our corporate strategy. Through rigorous quality control, advanced research, and precision-driven animal health management, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of purity, safety, and nutritional excellence.
With a fully integrated supply chain and an unwavering commitment to innovation and sustainability, we continue to deliver a premium portfolio of dairy products trusted by generations. Our drive for excellence keeps us at the forefront of the industry, shaping the future of dairy with confidence, resilience, and an uncompromising focus on quality.

Quality Control

Lanka Dairies Limited currently maintains an internationally recognized Food safety Management System (FSMS) that complies with ISO 22000 : 2018 requirements. This system ensures that stringent food safety standards are upheld at every stage—from sourcing raw materials to the storage of finished products. To maintain excellence, samples undergo meticulous testing in fully equipped chemical and microbiological laboratories before processing. Lanka Dairies Limited also operates its own Quality Assurance Division, which oversees environmental management, hygiene, and sanitation standards across all facilities. Additionally, an ultra-modern, fully automated packing plant, along with in-house laundry, water purification, and janitorial services, ensures superior control over quality and cleanliness at every stage of production

Research

Lanka Dairies Limited prioritizes research and development (R&D) as a key driver of continuous growth and innovation. Specialized research is conducted in artificial insemination to preserve and enhance superior cattle pedigree, ensuring a high-yielding and resilient herd.
The animal feed formulations are also developed in-house by the Lanka Dairies Limited R&D team, designed to maximize productivity while maintaining the highest quality standards across all dairy products. This science-driven approach ensures optimal nutrition for the herd, leading to superior milk yield and consistency.

Animal Health

At Lanka Dairies Limited, the health and well-being of our cattle are paramount. Our herds are provided with safe, well-maintained enclosures, high-quality feed, and carefully regulated environmental conditions to ensure optimal living standards.
Special attention is given to the care of pregnant and milking cows, with dedicated protocols in place to support their health and productivity. Leading this effort is an internationally experienced veterinarian, who heads a specialized healthcare team equipped to deliver proactive monitoring and timely medical interventions whenever necessary.
Our commitment to animal welfare, precision farming, and veterinary excellence ensures that our cattle remain healthy, resilient, and capable of producing the highest-quality milk.
