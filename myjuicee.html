My Juicee

My Juicee is a refreshing, ready-to-drink fruit beverage that not only satisfies your thirst but also provides your daily vitamin C requirement. Carefully crafted using high-quality ingredients, it is produced and aseptically packaged at Lanka Dairies’ cutting-edge facility in Welisara, ensuring the highest standards of hygiene and quality.
Available in apple, mango, mixed fruit, and orange variants, My Juicee offers a vibrant burst of flavor in every sip. Made from premium fruit pulps and concentrates sourced both locally and internationally, it adheres to stringent global quality standards.
Designed for all ages, My Juicee is the perfect blend of taste and nutrition. Whether enjoyed on the go with a 180ml pack or shared with family in a one-litre take-home pack, it delivers a refreshing and wholesome experience every time.
